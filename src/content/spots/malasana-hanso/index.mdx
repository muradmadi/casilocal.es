## First Impressions
HanSo cafe in Malasaña is a breath of fresh air in a neighborhood that's increasingly becoming a hipster haven. Tucked away on a quiet street just off the bustling Calle de Fuencarral, this cozy cafe is a great spot to escape the chaos of the barrio. Unlike the tourist traps near Sol, HanSo has a laid-back, de toda la vida vibe that's perfect for a casual coffee or meeting with friends. The exterior is unassuming, but the interior is stylish and modern, with a beautifully designed interior that's perfect for a quick snap for the 'gram.

## The Setup
The workspace at HanSo is comfortable, with a variety of seating options ranging from cozy armchairs to large wooden tables. The cafe has plenty of natural light, thanks to the large windows that look out onto the street, and the atmosphere is calm and relaxed. In terms of practicalities, HanSo has a decent number of power outlets, including a few USB ports, which are conveniently located near the windows and at the bar. The wifi is fast and reliable, making it a great spot to get some work done. However, it's worth noting that the cafe can get busy during the post-siesta rush, so it's best to arrive early to snag a good spot.

## The Coffee
The coffee at HanSo is top-notch, with a variety of specialty coffee drinks made with high-quality beans. The cafe con leche is rich and creamy, and the pour-overs are expertly crafted. The baristas are knowledgeable and friendly, and they're happy to chat about the different coffee options and make recommendations. The food options are also delicious, with a variety of tasty sandwiches, salads, and pastries. Be sure to try the tortilla de patatas, which is a classic Spanish dish done to perfection.

## The Verdict
Overall, HanSo cafe is a great spot for remote workers and coffee lovers alike. It's a great alternative to the usual Malasaña hipster nonsense, and the atmosphere is relaxed and welcoming. The coffee is excellent, and the food options are delicious. While it can get busy during peak hours, the cafe is generally quiet and comfortable, making it a great spot to get some work done or meet with friends. Just be sure to avoid the 11am café con leche crowd and the pre-vermut rush, and you'll be golden. With its convenient location, tasty coffee, and comfortable workspace, HanSo cafe is a must-visit for anyone looking for a great cafe experience in Malasaña.
