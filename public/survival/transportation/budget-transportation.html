Getting Around

Budget Transportation Guide for Students in Madrid

€20/Month Unlimited Travel

Getting around Madrid shouldn't eat your entire budget. With the right student passes and insider tricks, you can travel unlimited for €20/month or explore Spain on a shoestring. Here's how Madrid students actually get around without going broke.

The Madrid Student Metro Pass (Your Best Investment)

Abono Joven (Young Person's Pass)

⭐ Must Have
💰 €20/month 👤 Under 26 🗺️ ALL zones
Metro Buses Cercanías trains Light rail BiciMAD (30 min free)

What this means: Live in Getafe, study in Moncloa, party in Malasaña? All covered for €20. That's literally 10 single metro tickets.

📋 What You Need

  • Passport or NIE
  • Proof you're under 26
  • Student card (recommended)
  • €4 for the card itself
  • Passport photo (taken there)

📍 Where to Apply

  • Any metro customer service
  • Best: Sol, Nuevos Ministerios, Avenida América
  • Card ready in 10 minutes
💡 Pro tip: If you're turning 27 mid-semester, load your Abono Joven beforehand. It stays valid for the loaded months.

👴 Over 26? (Non-Student Option)

Regular Monthly Pass: €54.60/month (all zones)

Still cheaper than paying per ride (€1.50-2.00 each)

Madrid Metro System Basics

⏰ Operating Hours

  • Monday-Sunday: 6:00am - 1:30am
  • Night gap: 1:30am - 6:00am (use night buses)
  • 12 Metro Lines + Light Rail: Color-coded, impossible to get lost

Download: Metro Madrid app (free, offline maps, journey planner)

Fastest Routes Between Student Areas

Moncloa ↔ Sol Line 3 (direct, 10 min)
Lavapiés ↔ Malasaña Line 1 to Line 5 (15 min)
Sol ↔ Carlos III Metro L1 + Cercanías C4 (40 min)
Argüelles ↔ Gran Vía Line 3 (5 min)

⏰ Peak Hours to Avoid

8-9:30am, 6-8pm (packed with commuters)

🛡️ Safety Tips

  • Watch your belongings (pickpockets exist)
  • Keep backpack in front during rush hour
  • Emergency buttons in every car

Night Transportation (After Metro Closes)

Búhos (Night Buses)

🦉 Owls
⏰ 11:45pm - 6am 💰 FREE with Abono Joven
Green buses Routes start with "N" All pass Plaza de Cibeles

Key Routes for Students

N1 Moncloa → Sol → Atocha (main student route)
N2 Sol → Argüelles → Moncloa
N9 Malasaña → Lavapiés area
💡 Pro tip: Download EMT app for real-time bus arrival. Saves you freezing at stops.

Taxis & Ride Apps

🚕 Official Madrid Taxis

White with red stripe

Base fare: €2.50

Average: €8-15 for most routes

Green light = available

📱 Uber & Cabify

Usually similar price to taxis

Surge pricing: Can get expensive at 3am weekends

Compare all three before booking!

💡 The Night Strategy: Stay out until 6am metro reopens (saves taxi, classic Madrid move)

BiciMAD (City Bike Share)

BiciMAD

🚲 Electric Bikes
💰 FREE with Abono Joven (first 30 min) 📱 BiciMAD app

How to Use:

  1. Register on BiciMAD app
  2. Link your Abono Joven
  3. Find nearby station on app
  4. Unlock bike with app or card
  5. Return to any station within 30 min

✅ Best For

  • Short trips across neighborhoods
  • Nice weather days
  • Exploring city on weekends
  • Retiro Park rides

❌ Not Great For

  • Long commutes (30 min limit)
  • Hilly areas
  • Rainy days
  • Heavy backpacks
